Privacy Policy

1. Information we collect

Email address (sign in via email OTP, account recovery); Username (chosen by you, shown publicly on your reviews and listings); User-generated content (bathroom listings, reviews, saved places); Location at query time (when you use the map, your device's current location is sent with each search to find nearby bathrooms; we do not record location history — only the coordinates needed to answer that request are processed, not stored against your account).

2. How we use it

Authenticate you, show nearby bathrooms, attribute reviews/listings to your username, maintain your saved list. We do not sell personal information and do not use it for advertising.

3. Data storage & processors

Account and content data is stored and processed on our behalf by Supabase, Inc., our backend/database provider, acting as data processor. Auth tokens are stored securely on your device. Location coordinates submitted in a search are transmitted only to compute nearby results and are not retained as location history.

4. Your choices & rights

Edit your username anytime in Settings; sign out anytime; delete your account and associated data yourself, in-app, via Settings > Danger zone > Delete account (permanently removes profile, reviews, saved places; bathrooms you added remain listed but are no longer attributed to you); or email hello@toodaloo.app to request deletion or ask questions.

5. Children

Not directed at children under 13; we do not knowingly collect their data.
